Inspiration & Legacy
Choudhary
Rafal Singh Ji

Founding Inspiration · Haridwar, Uttarakhand

This trust stands as a living tribute to the vision and values of Choudhary Rafal Singh Ji — a man whose lifelong dedication to community, compassion, and justice planted the seeds of transformation across generations. His spirit lives in every child we educate, every family we heal, every well we dig.

"Serve the many, uplift the few, and leave the world better than you found it — that is the only legacy worth leaving."

His belief that every child deserves an education, every family deserves clean water, and every community deserves dignity lives on through the work we do each day.

About the Trust

Rooted in the
Soils of Uttarakhand

Founded and registered in Haridwar, Uttarakhand, the Choudhary Rafal Singh Educational Trust was established to honour the ideals of its founding inspiration by bringing tangible, sustainable change to underserved communities.

We operate with complete transparency, guided by a dedicated board and passionate volunteers who share one goal — a more equitable society for every person, regardless of their background.

Education

Scholarships, school resources & infrastructure for rural children

Healthcare

Free medical camps, awareness drives & hospital support

Clean Water

Purification systems & conservation across villages

Environment

Tree plantation, eco-awareness & sustainable practices
